## Implementation Status

DCGCL V0 has been implemented inside the nested BGRL harness:

- `bgrl/dcgcl_utils.py`: feature-only and propagation-view teachers, spherical k-means prototypes, soft assignments, disagreement gate, diagnostics, and soft contrastive loss.
- `diagnose_dcgcl_prototypes.py`: label-free prototype diagnostic without training the encoder.
- `train_dcgcl_transductive.py`: DCGCL transductive training entrypoint with semantic/topology dual heads.
- `reproduce_dcgcl.py`: sequential runner for diagnostics and training.

The implementation compiles and the runner dry-run passed. The reproducer was patched to decode child-process output as UTF-8 after a Windows GBK decode crash.

Known simplification: V0 uses one shared prototype bank over the mixed teacher embedding instead of separate aligned `C_x` / `C_a` banks from the proposal. This is acceptable for a first pilot but should be revisited if the method is redesigned.

## Decision

M2 does not pass. DCGCL V0 has a small Chameleon split-0 positive signal, but it regresses on Cora and Texas and only ties Wisconsin. This is not enough to justify 10-split expansion or external strong-baseline runs.

Recommended next action: redesign before further experiments. The most likely failure is that the current shared prototype bank and global soft positives over-regularize homophily and do not isolate the useful disagreement cases on Texas/Wisconsin. A minimal revision should test separate aligned feature/topology prototype banks plus a stricter high-confidence agreement mask before running M2 again.
