## Pass / Fail Rules

NPG split-0 is a pass only if:

1. Cora or CiteSeer improves over same-run control by at least `+0.01`;
2. Chameleon is non-negative or positive over same-run control;
3. Texas/Wisconsin do not show severe regression relative to BGRL graph branch;
4. gain weights do not collapse to all-uniform or all-minimum;
5. no NaN/collapse.

If R051 fails fairly, stop NPG and move to SC-BGRL. Do not tune NPG for more than one small ablation round unless the signal is close and interpretable.
