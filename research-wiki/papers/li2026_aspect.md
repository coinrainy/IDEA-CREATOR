# ASPECT

ASPECT is a recent node-level adaptive spectral fusion method for graph contrastive learning. It learns a node-wise spectral policy for low/high-frequency views and directly overlaps with the abandoned NFR-GCL direction.

## Relevance

ASPECT is required closest prior work for any node-wise spectral GCL method. SRLP-GCL should cite it and avoid claiming novelty as a node-level frequency routing method.
