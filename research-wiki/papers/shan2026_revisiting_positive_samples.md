# Revisiting Positive Samples in Graph Contrastive Learning

This recent work argues that message passing can trivialize positive sample maximization in graph contrastive learning, and proposes SPGCL to restore positive sample usefulness through Dirichlet-energy-aware design.

## Relevance

This paper is the strongest motivation for SRLP-GCL. SRLP-GCL should not claim to be the first to identify positive-sample trivialization; it should claim a complementary solution through residual latent target prediction.
