# Graph-JEPA

Graph-JEPA applies joint-embedding predictive architecture ideas to graph-level representation learning. It predicts latent representations of masked target subgraphs from context subgraphs, and was accepted by TMLR.

## Relevance

This is a direct prior for any graph JEPA-style method. SRLP-GCL must not claim novelty for generic graph latent prediction; its delta is node-level residual target construction for graph contrastive learning and heterophilous node classification.
