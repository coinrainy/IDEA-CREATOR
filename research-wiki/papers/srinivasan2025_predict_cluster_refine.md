# Predict, Cluster, Refine

This arXiv work proposes a non-contrastive joint embedding predictive framework for graph SSL, including context-to-multiple-target prediction and GMM-derived pseudo-label scoring.

## Relevance

It raises novelty risk for generic graph JEPA claims. SRLP-GCL should be framed as residual target construction plus contrastive residual discrimination for node classification, not as another general non-contrastive graph JEPA.
